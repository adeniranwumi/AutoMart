About Us

AutoCart was created with the vision to create a central marketplace for buyers and sellers of automobiles.

For this reason, we developed this platform with the intention to create a safe haven for car owners and consumers. This can be seen in our extra effort to prevent fraud and ensure only quality goods are displayed.

We also believe in the empowerment of every individual, which is why this site is hospitable to sellers as well as buyers to enourage entrepreneurship.

You can send us a message via our contact form or reach out to us on our social media handles.
